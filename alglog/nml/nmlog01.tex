\item forms: $\Gamma\pr{} \phi$, where $\Gamma$ -- a {\em sequence} of
$\prf$, $\phi\in\lrf$
\item the notion of a (program?) formula $B$ interfereing another (logical?)

$B\is x=...\Impl x\not\in\Vars\phi$
